Engineering privacy for vulnerable populations


Contributor(s)

Carmela Troncoso


Session

Cryptography and Privacy for the People


Abstract

In this talk, I will discuss different conceptualizations of privacy, and argue that focusing on data hiding and unauthorized flows limitation does not necessarily relate to protection. Instead, we need to focus on using technological advances to operationalize purpose limitation. I will give some examples of how this can be done.
