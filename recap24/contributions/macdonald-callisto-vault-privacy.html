Callisto Vault: Privacy and Cryptography in Action


Contributor(s)

Scott MacDonald


Session

Cryptography & Privacy for the People


Abstract

Callisto Vault provides survivors of sexual assault a way to identify the perpetrator of their assault and see if they assaulted anyone else. In this talk, we discuss how cryptography is used to protect the privacy of survivors and perpetrators alike.
