crypto_doodles: cryptography through comics and jokes


Contributor(s)

Eysa Lee


Session

Dreaming and Design


Abstract

What started as a way of live-tweeting cute moments during presentations, crypto_doodles became a way of documenting cryptography-related jokes, from puns, to fun talk interactions, to questions about motivation slides (why DOES Bob have so many laptops?). This submission looks at highlights of crypto_doodles and the conversations started from these doodles.
